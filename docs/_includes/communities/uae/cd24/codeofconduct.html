Code of Conduct

  1. Respect and Inclusivity

    Treat all attendees, speakers, sponsors, and staff with respect and consideration. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

  2. Constructive Participation

    Engage in discussions and activities with a constructive and collaborative mindset. Share knowledge and ideas in a manner that is supportive and enriching.

  3. Professionalism

    Maintain a professional demeanor throughout the event. This includes being punctual, prepared, and respectful in all interactions.

  4. Compliance with Venue Policies

    Adhere to all rules and regulations set forth by the event venue. Respect the property, facilities, and resources provided.

  5. Health and Safety

    Prioritize the health and safety of yourself and others. Follow any event-specific health guidelines and protocols.

  6. Reporting and Resolution

    If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to event staff or organizers as soon as possible.

By participating in AWS Community Day 2024, you are helping us create a positive and respectful environment for all. Thank you for your cooperation and commitment to these standards.
